About Us

WeAware(Empowering Women by Creating Awareness)

Raising awareness around the causes of poor health and wellbeing that are related to gender. Empowering women and girls through water, sanitation and hygiene; health and nutrition; livelihoods training; and education programs to increase their opportunities and ability to reach their full potential.

Education, training, awareness raising, building self-confidence, expansion of choices, increased access to and control over resources, and actions to transform the structures and institutions that reinforce and perpetuate gender discrimination and inequality are important tools for empowering women and girls to claim their rights.We spread awareness about all these scheme and make it simple.
